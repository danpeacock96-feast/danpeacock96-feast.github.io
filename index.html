Little Thetford Village Feast 28th August 2021 12-4pm

The 2021 Feast

The Feast Committee are excited to welcome everyone back to our village event after a year’s break.

More than ever, our focus for the Feast is “bringing our community together”.
The day kicks off from noon with some returning favourites, like the Bottle Tombola, Coconut Shy, Hang Tough, the traditional Adult and Children’s Competitions, Kids Races, BBQ, Bar, Tea, Coffee and Cake, Raffle and a number of stallholders. Children will be able to enjoy a Magician, Bouncy Castle, Obstacle Course, Hook a Duck, Story Telling Corner and lots more…



A particularly special event for this year’s Feast will be the official opening of “Bert’s Garden” at 12:30.
 Bert was a larger than life figure in the village and has been part of its history for almost a century. To commemorate his life and contribution to Little Thetford the Feast Committee has come together to create a memorial garden for everyone to enjoy and remember him by.

Benefitting the Community

Throughout the last year the funds from previous feasts have been used to benefit the community.

Bert's Garden

The Feast Committee, along with volunteers, have worked hard to create a garden for everyone to enjoy within the village in honor of Bert. There will be an official opening at this years feast.

Speed Signs

Funds from previous events have gone towards implementing speed signs in the village as a safety measure.

Defibrillator

The Feast committee has raised funds for the village defibrillator (located at the village hall).
