Benefitting the Community

Throughout the last year the funds from previous feasts have been used to benefit the community.

Bert's Garden

The Feast Committee, along with volunteers, have worked hard to create a garden for everyone to enjoy within the village in honor of Bert. There will be an official opening at this years feast.

Speed Signs

Funds from previous events have gone towards implementing speed signs in the village as a safety measure.

Defibrillator

The Feast committee has raised funds for the village defibrillator (located at the village hall).
